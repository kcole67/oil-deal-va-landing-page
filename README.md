## 📌 Overview

The **Oil Deal VA Command Center Landing Page** is designed to attract, educate, and convert virtual assistants, freelancers, and researchers into trained oil & energy deal sourcing specialists.

This page introduces a structured system that helps users:

* Learn how to source real oil & energy deals
* Understand industry roles (buyers, sellers, mandates)
* Qualify and organize high-value leads

---

## 🎯 Purpose

This landing page serves as the **front-end entry point** for:

* Recruiting virtual assistants
* Capturing leads (email / waitlist)
* Showcasing the Oil Deal VA training system
* Driving users into the full command center platform

---

## 🧠 Target Audience

* Virtual Assistants
* Freelancers
* Remote Workers
* LinkedIn Prospectors
* Lead Researchers
* Individuals looking to learn high-income research skills

---

## 💡 Core Value Proposition

> Train VAs to find **real oil & energy deal opportunities** — not fake leads, not guesswork.

Users will learn how to:

* Identify real participants in oil transactions
* Research companies and contacts properly
* Filter out low-quality or fake leads
* Organize qualified opportunities
